About My Research Experiences

I am Mashfiq and I completed my undergraduate studies in 2021 from Brac University, Bangladesh. My undergraduate thesis titled as "Facial Expression Recognition: Convolutional Attentional Masking Network and Ensemble Deep Learning Model", under the supervision of Professor Kabir (Google Scholar profile link) , has proposed a new variant of the ResNet model named as Convolutional Attentional Masking Network (CAMnet) that simultaneously uses the split attentional module and the masking module mechanisms. While doing the thesis, I have learnt about linear algebra, calculus, probability, statistics, supervised and unsupervised algorithms, gradient descent, bias and variance, underfitting and overfitting, gradient vanishing, some transfer learning techniques such as fine tuning, features extraction , various optimization algorithms namely SGD, Adam, AdaDelta, RAdam, architectures of deep learning models, advantages and disadvantages of a model. Furthermore, I have contributed significantly in various processes such as data preprocessing and augmentation, regularization, normalization, hyperparameter tuning.

During my undergraduate studies, I along with my other group members published a conference paper titled as "An Algorithmic Approach to Driver Drowsiness Detection for Ensuring Safety in an Autonomous Car" (paper link) under the supervision of Professor Islam (Google Scholar profile link) in 2020 IEEE Region 10 Symposium. After conducting further research and improvement, a journal paper titled as "A Novel Approach to Enhance Safety on Drowsy Driving in Self-Driving Car" has been submitted to Springer Mobile Networks and Applications (MONET) and it is currently under final review process. The main contributions of these researches are an algorithmic approach which can be implemented in the existing autonomous vehicles, that can extract various facial features for detecting drowsiness and proposed ensemble drowsiness detection model achieves a significantly higher drowsiness detection accuracy of 98 percent. Also, the proposed model has the functionality of detecting a free parking space and also navigating towards the chosen free parking space which further enhances human safety.

I have also completed various projects such as object detection and avoidance robot, line follower robot based on Webots, C++, Tinkercad, also various machine learning based projects such as Titanic Survivors Prediction, Breast Cancer Detection, House Price Prediction, Mushroom Classification etc. I have also developed a food ordering management mobile application based on Java, HTML, CSS , a recipe showing web application based on JavaScript, a to do web app using REACT JS. Furthermore, also some there are some projects based on Python such as Guessing Game, Graph Theory, 8 Queens Game, Drowsiness Detection etc which I have completed during my undergraduate studies. My primary research interests include Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Data Science, and Computer Vision.
